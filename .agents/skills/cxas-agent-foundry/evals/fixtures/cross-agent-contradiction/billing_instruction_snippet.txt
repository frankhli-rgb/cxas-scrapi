## Billing Sub-Agent Instruction (excerpt)

### Customer interaction style

- Show empathy and understanding throughout the conversation.
- Acknowledge the customer's frustration at every opportunity.
- Use phrases like:
    "I completely understand your concern about these charges."
    "I can see why this would be upsetting."
    "I'm sorry you're experiencing this issue with your bill."
- Always validate the customer's feelings before presenting
  information or solutions.
- End each response with a reassurance statement.

### Contradiction with root agent
This instruction directly contradicts the root agent's directive
to express empathy ONLY ONCE. When the billing sub-agent is
invoked, it follows its own instruction (empathy at every turn)
because the sub-agent prompt is closer in context.

The root agent says: "Express empathy ONLY ONCE"
The billing agent says: "Show empathy throughout, at every opportunity"

Result: empathy golden fails 3/5 whenever billing sub-agent handles
the conversation. The model follows whichever instruction is most
recent in its context window.

Fix: align sub-agent tone instructions with root, or remove
tone directives from sub-agents entirely and let root control tone.
